managing the computer resource a stage hypothesis based on the study of expenditures for data processing a descriptive stage hypothesis is presented it is suggested that the planning organizing and controlling activities associated with managing the computer resource will change in character over a period of time and will evolve in patterns roughly correlated to four stages of the computer budget stage i computer acquisition stage ii intense system development stage iii proliferation of controls and stage iv user/service orientation each stage is described and related to individual tasks for managing the computer resource cacm july 1973 nolan r l computer management computer budget computer expenditures stage hypothesis planning organizing control computer resource 2.41 2.43 3.50 ca730701 jb january 23,1978 1:23 pm
